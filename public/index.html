TEXT MUSIC

Text music is an app that takes the text you input - any text - written, copy/pasted, or otherwise generated and turns it into music when you hit play. Then you can speed it up or slow it down using the tempo slider.

Try It!

Instructions

  1. Enter a passage of text in the bottom field, and a title in the top
  2. Hit the play button to listen to your text
  3. Edit as you like and hit add to save it
